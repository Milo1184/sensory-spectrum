Location

New Jersey

The 12,000 square foot testing facility is designed to meet the qualitative and quantitative research needs of consumer product companies. Our facility includes two large multipurpose rooms with viewing capabilities, two focus group suites with viewing and audio video capabilities, which include onsite capture to DVD and web streaming for real time viewing from other locations, a commercial and residential kitchen for all your food preparation needs, 12 sinks and six showers for testing health and beauty aid products, six odor booths and a training room for courses and workshops. Recruiting is managed on site via internet and phone.

Descriptive analysis and discrimination testing are also available to assist you. Learn about your product and gain consumer understanding by utilizing our services through complete handson sensory experiences.

Centrally located in northern New Jersey, near NYC, Short Hills, Morristown, this state-of the art facility is 14 miles from Newark Airport and 26 miles from midtown Manhattan.

North Carolina

Sensory Spectrum - Spectrum Discovery Center NC is part of the North Carolina Research Campus located in Kannapolis, just 30 minutes north of Charlotte. It is designed to mirror the capabilities of the Sensory Spectrum - Spectrum Discovery Center NJ, with comparable services and expertise. Sensory Spectrum consultants will be at both locations ensuring research will continue to be uniquely designed to satisfy the varied needs of you, our clients.

The 11,000 square foot testing facility is designed to meet your needs for qualitative and quantitative research, trained panels for food and non food evaluations, sinks for health, home and beauty aid product testing.

Our facility includes two focus group suites with viewing and audio video capabilities, a large multipurpose room, a commercial and residential kitchen for all your food preparation needs and 10 sinks for testing health and beauty aid products.
