Method of Payment

An early registration discount of 10% is offered for all courses if the fee is paid 6 weeks prior to the course date. We also offer a 25% discount on the second registration when two people from the same company register for the same course, at the same time. The early registration discount and the second registration discount cannot be combined. Registration information for the Sensory Evaluation Course may be found at www.cfpa.com (Sensory Spectrums early registration and group discounts do not apply for this course).

Course fee includes lunch each day, dinner the first night of multiday courses, course notebook, appropriate texts and demonstration supplies. Cancellation Policy for all courses:

  • Registration fee is fully refundable on cancellation made 10 business days prior to course start date.
  • Registrants canceling 5-9 business days prior to start date are subject to a $150 service charge.
  • Registrants who fail to attend or cancel less than 5 days prior to the seminar start date are responsible for the entire fee. Substitutions may be made at any time.

Sensory Spectrum reserves the right to modify the content, change instructors, or cancel the courses.
